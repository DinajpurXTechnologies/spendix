Privacy Policy

1. Data Collection

SpendiX collects personal information you provide during account registration, including your name, email address, and profile photo. We also collect financial data you enter such as income, expenses, and bill details.

2. User Information

Your information is used solely to provide the SpendiX service. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data to any third party. Your financial records are private and only accessible to you.

3. Firebase Usage

SpendiX uses Google Firebase for authentication, real-time database storage, and file storage. Firebase is GDPR-compliant and employs industry-standard security measures. Data is stored in secure Google Cloud servers.

4. Authentication Data

Authentication is handled by Firebase Authentication. Passwords are never stored in plain text and are managed securely by Firebase. Google Sign-In uses OAuth 2.0 for secure authentication.

5. Storage Usage

Profile photos are stored in Firebase Storage. Financial transaction data, bills, and settings are stored in Firebase Realtime Database, accessible only by you through your authenticated account.

6. Security

All data transmission is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS. Firebase Database Rules ensure each user can only read/write their own data. We regularly review and update our security practices.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, export, or delete all your personal data at any time through the Profile section of the app. You may also request account deletion, which permanently removes all your data.

8. Data Deletion

To delete your account and all associated data, go to Profile → Delete Account. This action is irreversible and will permanently erase all transactions, bills, and personal information from our servers.

9. Contact Information

For privacy concerns or data requests, contact us at: www.spendix.in or through DinajpurX Technologies. Last updated: January 2026.
